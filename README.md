# airdraw

Draw on screen by pinching your fingers in the air. Your webcam tracks your hand with MediaPipe and pinching thumb to index finger allows you to draw. Runs entirely in the browser, no backend.

Hosted at [airdraw-cat.vercel.app](https://airdraw-cat.vercel.app)

## Use

Pinch your thumb and index finger together to draw and open the pinch to move without drawing. The toolbar has color options, an eraser, a color picker (select it, hover the image to preview the color, then pinch to grab it), an RGB color picker, a brush size slider, a background toggle (camera/white/black), a camera picker, and per-stroke undo/redo and clear. Export PNG opens a preview where you can swap between camera, background, and transparent before downloading.
